Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

What we collect

  • Account info — email address and full name when you sign up.
  • Location — your device location to show nearby photographers and set meeting points. Used only while the app is in use.
  • Photos — photographers upload shoot photos to deliver them to clients. Clients may save photos to their device.
  • Payment info — card details are handled entirely by Stripe and never stored on our servers.
  • Push token — to send booking and status notifications.

How we use it

  • Match clients with nearby photographers.
  • Process and track bookings and payments.
  • Deliver photos from photographer to client.
  • Send notifications about your orders.

What we share

  • Stripe — processes all payments. See stripe.com/privacy.
  • Supabase — our database and storage provider. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data retention

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab. This permanently removes your account and associated data.
